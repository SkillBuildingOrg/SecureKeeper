General Information:

Secure Keeper values your privacy. This application is created by the group of enthusiast developers to address some of the daily needs like one have to remember different credentials/access-codes/tokens for different accounts or places to access. This application can be used to remember all the codes and respective accounts/places which uses it. User need to set-up the master password for using the application and then add the data which user wants to remember/access. This is secured applications for storing, accessing and safeguarding your personal information. 'Secure Keeper' has created this Privacy Policy to indicate our commitment to protecting your privacy. It also gives you the choices to organize user's personal information and how it can be updated.

We reserve the right to change this Policy. If we make any material changes we will notify you by means of a notice on this Site prior to the change becoming effective. If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at sinfinitystudio@gmail.com

Security

Your 'Secure Keeper' data/records and profile data are password-protected so that you have secure access to update that information. It is the user's responsibility to protect the security of their master password and backup files. Secure Keeper stores all information inside application in encrypted format.

Information We Collect

Secure Keeper is completely off-line application and does not sends/collects any personal information from your device. The profile which user sets up is also stand alone and not shared with anyone.

Secure Keeper will never have access to your Master Password or individual records stored within your Keeper.

Passwords

Access to Secure Keeper are protected by password that is known by you only. Secure Keeper has designed security processes that encrypt customer passwords to protect it from being divulged or accessed by anyone other than you. Neither Secure Keeper developers nor any of its contractors can obtain or access your password. Secure Keeper developers or nor any of its contractors will ask you for your password via email or telephone nor any other unsolicited manner.

Import/Export data

Secure Keeper exports data in encrypted format and stores on device memory. The contents of the files are encrypted and readable only inside the application.

Further Questions

If you have further questions about our Privacy Policy, email us at sinfinitystudio@gmail.com.
